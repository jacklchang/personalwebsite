My 5 core values

  1. Reflection

    2. ​This value means to me a look upon my actions in the past and evaluate as to whether they were positively impactful or negatively impactful. This gives me an opportunity to learn as to what I can improve upon for the next situation, or praise myself on a good deed and take the time to appreciate myself. This value is the most important one to me because my goal is to constantly strive to be a good person and to reflect on myself is critical to achieving this goal.

  2. Authenticity

    3. ​This value means to present myself in a way that is true. I find that being true to myself and being unafraid to express that is a great privilege. I hope to inspire a safe environment that allows others to be true to themselves as well. This value is extremely important to me because I find that by being authentic, I can make deeper connections with others.

  3. Connection

    4. This value means to agree with someone on values, interests and more. I have been very lucky to meet some people I have a deep connection with and the bond created from it is so special. I hope to create an environment as a leader to which everyone can connect with each other in some way and this promotes a healthy work environment. For example, I would be willing to go the extra mile for someone I have a connection with - whether that is staying up late at night to help them with their assignments or always picking up their phone calls. I find genuine and pure connections are what makes a team cohesive and leads to better results.

  4. Perceptiveness

    5. This value means to be aware of my surroundings and other people’s behaviour. I find this value important because there have been situations in which someone has been offended by a comment made and being attentive to their body language allows there to be an opportunity to learn, apologize and overcome the situation. I highly value being perceptive, because I hope to always take note on someone’s state of mind and if it is negative I would always want to do my best to turn it around.

  5. Efficiency

    6. ​This value means performing tasks at the highest quality in the shortest amount of time. I find this value important to me because I do not like to take the long way around things. For example, I organize my grocery list based on the location of the items in the store so I do not need to go back and forth between aisles. I find that this saves me time and I highly enjoy being efficient.
