Being a Better Ally

I had the privilege and pleasure in sharing my story to 2 groups of 30 participants on being a better ally.

My biggest takeaways from this experience

  • Be careful with the terminology, make sure to get a full understanding of the word and possible connotations before using it.
  • It is a fine line between empathizing with someone's experience and invalidating them.
  • I need to learn to be okay with awkward silences when asking the audience a question - maybe sing Happy Birthday in my head.
  • We need to create spaces that are geared towards learning and allowing to make mistakes.

Note: Thank you to my mentors Cynthia, Dawn and Megan for supporting and guiding me through the process. Thank you to the ECC DLC Committee for organizing an amazing event. Thank you to all the participants who came to the workshop!
